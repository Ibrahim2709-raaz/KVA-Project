Program Information

Age Groups & Formation

Teams are organized by birth year (U12, U14, U16, U18) following provincial guidelines. Roster sizes target 10–12 athletes. Final formation considers skills evaluation, positional balance, and coach availability.

  • Tryouts held each August–September
  • Evaluation rubric: technical, tactical, physical, mindset
  • Mid-season movement only by director approval

Commitment Expectations

Our culture values attendance, communication, and growth. Families commit for the full season (Oct–Apr).

  • Training: 2–3 sessions/week, plus strength blocks
  • Tournaments: 5–8 events, travel required for some divisions
  • Player code: be coachable, arrive early, own your gear & recovery
  • Parent code: support, not sideline coaching; respect officials

Practice Players & Team Structure

Practice players train with squads, access development plans, and may be called up for competition when eligible.

  • Squad model: A/Black (performance), B/Orange (development)
  • Shared curriculum across squads for seamless call-ups
  • Practice players: reduced fees, quarterly reviews, pathway to roster
